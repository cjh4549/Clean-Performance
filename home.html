What is Restoration?

A restoration company is a specialized business that provides services to repair, restore, and renovate properties that have been damaged by natural disasters, accidents, and other catastrophic events. Restoration companies typically offer a wide range of services, including water damage restoration, fire damage restoration, mold remediation, and biohazard cleanup.

Professional Service

Restoration companies employ trained professionals who have expertise in a variety of areas, such as water extraction, structural drying, smoke and soot damage cleanup, and mold remediation. They use specialized equipment and techniques to ensure that the restoration process is done quickly, efficiently, and safely.

Customer Satisfaction

One of the things that sets Clean Performance Restoration apart from other restoration companies is their commitment to customer satisfaction and safety. They carry a 2 million dollar liability insurance policy, which provides peace of mind to clients and ensures that they are fully protected in the event of any accidents or damage that may occur during the restoration process.

Renovation

In addition to their restoration services, Clean Performance Restoration also offers remodeling and renovation services to help clients restore and improve their properties. From small upgrades to major renovations, their team can help clients achieve their vision for their property.
